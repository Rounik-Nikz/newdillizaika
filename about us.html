ABOUT US

Once upon a time, in the bustling streets of Thane, there was a restaurant unlike any other. It wasn't just a place to eat; it was a culinary journey, a celebration of flavors, and a legacy of taste. This is the story of our restaurant, where every dish is crafted with love and every meal is an experience to remember. At our restaurant, we believe in creating dishes that you crave, dishes that linger in your memory long after the meal is over. Our menu is a testament to this belief, offering a delightful array of Indian dishes sure to tantalize your taste buds. From the rich and creamy Butter Chicken to the spicy and aromatic Biryani, each dish is a masterpiece, created with the perfect blend of spices and flavors. But what sets us apart is our commitment to quality. We create our spice mixes in-house, ensuring every dish has the freshest and finest ingredients. Quality is our priority, and we never compromise on it. Whether you choose to dine in or take away, you can rest assured that your meal will be nothing short of perfection. Our air-conditioned dine-in area provides a comfortable and inviting atmosphere, perfect for enjoying a meal with family and friends. And if you're in a hurry, our takeaway option allows you to enjoy our delicious dishes on the go. But our restaurant is not just about the food; it's about the experience. We cater to all events and occasions, from weddings to corporate events, birthday celebrations to baby showers. Our outdoor catering services are perfect for those who want to add a touch of culinary excellence to their special day. Since 2014, we have been serving our customers with love and dedication, and we continue to do so every day. Our legacy is one of taste, quality, and happiness. So come, join us on a journey of flavors, and let us create a recipe of happiness for you.

KNOW STORY BEHIND

The New Dilli Zaika Restaurant in Thane represents a heartfelt homage to Delhi's storied culinary heritage, meticulously curated to transport diners into the heart of India's capital city through its flavors. Located strategically in Thane, New Dilli Zaika offers a menu that intricately weaves together traditional Mughlai and Indian delicacies with contemporary twists inspired by global culinary trends. Each dish is a labor of love, prepared using authentic recipes, premium ingredients, and time-honored cooking techniques that pay homage to Delhi's diverse culinary traditions. The inspiration behind New Dilli Zaika stems from a profound admiration for Delhi's bustling markets, historical kitchens, and the cultural mosaic that defines the city. The founders, deeply rooted in their passion for preserving and promoting authentic flavors, have meticulously curated a menu that captures the essence of Delhi's vibrant street food culture and royal culinary legacies. Whether it's the rich aromas of slow-cooked Mughlai curries or the tantalizing flavors of tandoori specialties cooked in traditional clay ovens, each dish at New Dilli Zaika tells a story of tradition, innovation, and dedication to culinary excellence. Beyond offering an exceptional dining experience, New Dilli Zaika in Thane aims to create a welcoming ambiance where every visit is not just about enjoying a meal but about immersing oneself in the cultural tapestry of Delhi's gastronomic identity. From the warm hospitality to the carefully curated ambiance, every aspect of New Dilli Zaika reflects a commitment to delivering an authentic taste of Delhi's culinary legacy, ensuring that each guest leaves with a memorable experience and a deeper appreciation for the flavors that define India's capital city.
